Urology

Apple Hospitals has one of the best facilities for urology and kidney transplantation in Tanuku. We are equipped with state of the art pre and post operative care facilities which are designed to world standards. Patients who are suffering from various types of urological problems have been successfully cured due the innovative steps taken by our doctors in acting at the right time with the best equipment.

Our urology department functions with the assistance of various other departments including neprhology, Anesthesia and intensive care facilities. By using fully equipped operating theaters which are designed to international standards, we are able to successfully reduce the number of failed surgeries and also improve patient recovery timelines.







A few of the services which are offered by Tata Hospital include

* Laproscopy

* Prostrate Resection

* Bladder stone removal

* Reconstructive and incontinence surgeries

* Lithotripsy

* Identification of Urinary tract infections and their treatment
