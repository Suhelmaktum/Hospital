Paediatrics

Our Hospital offers one of the finest paediatric treatment services for children of different ages. We have highly specialized paediatric services which are required by patients who have various complications. With a highly specialized child care and trauma centre, we handle even the most critical cases with a high success rate for all patients.

Our hospital handles hundreds of cases every year in our paediatrics department and has a specialized team of Paediatric surgeons, consultants and visiting specialists who handle the need of our most sensitive patients. With a special emphasis on providing a stress free environment, we have designed our hospital environs to provide a soothing and relaxing experience for our child patients and remove any traces of stress during their treatment process.







Our pediatric surgical services include

* General Surgery

* Paediatric Urology Surgery

* Burns Surgery

* Laproscopic surgery

* Neonatal Surgery

* Thoracic Surgery
